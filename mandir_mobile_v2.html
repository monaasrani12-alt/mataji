Shreepriya Navjyot Mataji is the devoted spiritual head and guiding force of Shree Navjyot Mataji Mandir. Through years of unwavering faith and compassion, she has built a community rooted in love, devotion and seva.

Her vision goes beyond prayer — she believes that a mandir must serve not just the soul, but also the lives of those around it.

About the Mandir

🕌 A Sacred Space

A humble yet radiant temple devoted to Maa Jagdamba — the divine embodiment of Shakti, compassion and protection for the whole community.

💛 Community at Heart

Lovingly serving the families of Bhandup West — a spiritual home that welcomes every soul with warmth and devotion.

🙏 Tuesday Prayer Evenings

Every Tuesday evening, devotees gather for soulful prayer meets that fill the heart with peace, unity and divine grace.

🌸 Festivals Full of Life

Navratri and every festival is celebrated with grandeur, music and deep community participation — faith at its most joyful.

Five Pillars of Seva

🩺 Health & Wellness

Free health camps, women's awareness sessions & local doctor partnerships bringing care to every doorstep.

📚 Education Support

School supplies, career guidance & English workshops opening doors for the next generation.

🍱 Annadaan & Nutrition

Monthly ration kits for families in need; festive food distribution during Navratri & Diwali.

👩 Women Empowerment

Skill-building, financial literacy & self-help group support giving women confidence & independence.

✨ Values & Spiritual Growth

Youth sessions on gratitude, discipline & purpose; bhajan evenings with meaningful social messages.
